Welcome, brave adventurers, to the enchanting realm of MyTrickStEr, where magic and mystery converge in the sprawling expanse known as The Royal Continent. As you step into this mesmerizing world, prepare to embark on an epic journey that transcends the boundaries of imagination.

Explore The Royal Continent!

The Royal Continent is a land teeming with untold secrets and undiscovered wonders, awaits your exploration. From dense enchanted forests to towering mountains shrouded in mystic mists, every corner of this vast domain is infused with the essence of ancient magic. As you traverse its diverse landscapes, you will encounter mythical creatures, ancient ruins, and hidden realms waiting to be unveiled.

The Towns of Royal Continent

Lymhurst

Located in the forests of the Royal Continent, Lymhurst blends nature and civilization seamlessly. Bustling with markets and quests, it provides adventurers with essentials while retaining a mystical connection to the enchanted woods.

Bridgewatch

In the scorching heat of Royal Continent's desert lies an oasis town. Bridgewatch offers adventurers respite with its busy markets and exotic creatures. Explore the ancient secrets of Royal Continent's desert. But beware of its many dangers!

Fort Sterling

In the snowy mountains of the Royal Continent, sits an ancient fort built by the old greats. Fort Sterling has provided the people of Mount Brok with shelter and heat for many generations. Journey into the mystery and rediscover it's secrets.
