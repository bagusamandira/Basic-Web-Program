The Towns of The Royal Continent

Lymhurst

Located in the forests of the Royal Continent, Lymhurst blends nature and civilization seamlessly. Bustling with markets and quests, it provides adventurers with essentials while retaining a mystical connection to the enchanted woods.

Bridgewatch

In the scorching heat of Royal Continent's desert lies an oasis town. Bridgewatch offers adventurers respite with its busy markets and exotic creatures. Explore the ancient secrets of Royal Continent's desert. But beware of its many dangers!

Fort Sterling

In the snowy mountains of the Royal Continent, sits an ancient fort built by the old greats. Fort Sterling has provided the people of Mount Brok with shelter and heat for many generations. Journey into the mystery and rediscover it's secrets.
